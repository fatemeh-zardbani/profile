I am Fatemeh Zardbani, a researcher at Aarhus University working in database systems. My research focuses on adaptive indexing, spatial data management, and high-dimensional data processing, with an emphasis on practical performance and scalability.

Recent work explores:
- Adaptive indexing for spatial and metric data.
- High-dimensional clustering and benchmarking.
- Incremental maintenance of multi-dimensional data structures.
